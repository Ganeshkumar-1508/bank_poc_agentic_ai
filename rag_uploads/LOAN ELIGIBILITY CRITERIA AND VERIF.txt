LOAN ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA AND VERIFICATION REQUIREMENTS
=========================================================
Document Reference: LEC-2024-004
Effective Date: January 2024
Version: 2.2
Department: Loan Origination

1. GENERAL ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS
====================================

1.1 Age and Identity Requirements
----------------------------------
All loan applicants must meet the following minimum criteria:

  - Minimum age: 18 years old (21 years old for loans exceeding $50,000)
  - Must be a US citizen, permanent resident, or have valid visa with work authorization
  - Must have a valid Social Security Number (SSN) or Individual Taxpayer Identification Number (ITIN)
  - Must present government-issued photo identification (driver's license, passport, or state ID)
  - Must not be listed on OFAC Specially Designated Nationals (SDN) list

1.2 Residency Requirements
----------------------------
  - Must have a residential address in the United States (no P.O. boxes accepted)
  - Minimum time at current residence: 6 months (exceptions with supporting documentation)
  - Mailing address must match residential address unless documented reason provided
  - For mortgage applications: property must be located in the United States

1.3 Employment Requirements
------------------------------
  - Must be currently employed (minimum 3 months at current employer)
  - Self-employed applicants must provide 2 years of tax returns and business financials
  - Retired applicants must provide pension/retirement income documentation
  - Students may apply with co-signer (minimum FICO 700 for co-signer)
  - Unemployment benefits are NOT accepted as qualifying income

2. INCOME VERIFICATION STANDARDS
==================================

2.1 Acceptable Income Documentation
------------------------------------
Full Documentation (for loans above $25,000):
  - Most recent 2 years of W-2 forms
  - Most recent 2 months of pay stubs
  - Most recent 2 years of federal tax returns (all schedules)
  - Year-to-date pay stub showing year-to-date earnings

Simplified Documentation (for loans up to $25,000):
  - Most recent 2 months of pay stubs
  - Most recent W-2 form
  - Bank statements showing income deposits for 3 months

Self-Employment Documentation:
  - Most recent 2 years of personal and business tax returns (all schedules)
  - Year-to-date profit and loss statement
  - Business bank statements for 12 months
  - CPA letter confirming business operations (for loans above $50,000)

2.2 Income Calculation Methodology
------------------------------------
Qualifying Income Sources:
  - Base salary or hourly wages (calculated at 40 hours/week for hourly)
  - Overtime and bonuses: Only if received for 2+ consecutive years, averaged over 24 months
  - Commission income: Only if received for 2+ consecutive years, averaged over 24 months
  - Rental income: Net rental income (75% of gross rent for investment properties)
  - Investment income: Documented dividends and interest (averaged over 24 months)
  - Social Security income: Full amount acceptable
  - Pension income: Full amount acceptable
  - Alimony/child support: Only with court order and 12-month receipt history
  - Trust income: Must be documented with trust agreement

Non-Qualifying Income (NOT accepted):
  - Temporary or seasonal employment income
  - Unemployment benefits
  - Student financial aid
  - Gift funds (except for down payment on mortgage)
  - One-time bonuses or severance pay
  - Income from illegal sources

2.3 Minimum Income Requirements by Loan Type
----------------------------------------------
Personal Loan: Minimum $24,000 annual gross income
Auto Loan: Minimum $18,000 annual gross income
Mortgage: Minimum $36,000 annual gross income
Home Equity: Minimum $36,000 annual gross income
Small Business Loan: Minimum $60,000 annual gross income (personal + business)
Debt Consolidation: Minimum $30,000 annual gross income

3. LOAN-SPECIFIC ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
=======================================

3.1 Personal Loan Eligibility
-------------------------------
  - Loan Amount: $1,000 - $50,000
  - Term: 12 - 84 months
  - Minimum FICO: 640
  - Maximum DTI: 43%
  - Employment: Minimum 6 months at current employer
  - Income: Minimum $24,000 annually
  - Purpose: Debt consolidation, home improvement, medical expenses, major purchases, wedding, moving expenses
  - Prohibited purposes: Investment, gambling, illegal activities, business use (use business loan instead)

3.2 Mortgage Eligibility
--------------------------
  - Loan Amount: $50,000 - $1,000,000 (jumbo up to $3,000,000)
  - Term: 15 or 30 years (ARM products: 5/1, 7/1, 10/1)
  - Minimum FICO: 620 (conventional), 580 (FHA), 620 (VA)
  - Maximum DTI: 43% (QM compliant), 50% with compensating factors
  - LTV Maximum: 95% (conventional), 96.5% (FHA), 100% (VA)
  - PMI Required: If LTV exceeds 80% for conventional loans
  - Appraisal: Required from approved appraiser
  - Property Type: Single-family, condo, townhouse, 2-4 unit residential
  - Occupancy: Owner-occupied primary residence, second home, investment (rate adjusted)

3.3 Auto Loan Eligibility
---------------------------
  - Loan Amount: $5,000 - $100,000
  - Term: 24 - 84 months (new), 24 - 72 months (used)
  - Minimum FICO: 625 (used), 660 (new)
  - Maximum DTI: 45%
  - Vehicle Age: Maximum 10 model years old at loan maturity
  - Vehicle Mileage: Maximum 120,000 miles at purchase
  - Down Payment: Minimum 10% for used vehicles, 5% for new vehicles
  - Insurance: Comprehensive and collision insurance required for loan term
  - Title: Lienholder must be listed on title

4. DOCUMENT CHECKLIST BY LOAN TYPE
====================================

4.1 Personal Loan Required Documents
--------------------------------------
  - Government-issued photo ID
  - Most recent W-2 form
  - 2 most recent pay stubs
  - Bank statements (2 most recent months)
  - Signed loan application (Form 1003 equivalent)
  - Authorization to pull credit report
  - Proof of residence (utility bill or lease agreement)

4.2 Mortgage Required Documents
---------------------------------
  - Government-issued photo ID (all borrowers)
  - Social Security card (all borrowers)
  - 2 years W-2 forms (all borrowers)
  - 2 years federal tax returns with all schedules (all borrowers)
  - 2 most recent pay stubs (all borrowers)
  - 2 months bank statements (all accounts, all pages)
  - Signed loan application (Form 1003)
  - Purchase agreement (if purchase) or mortgage statement (if refinance)
  - Homeowner's insurance declaration page
  - Gift letter (if applicable, with donor bank statements)
  - Divorce decree / separation agreement (if applicable)
  - Bankruptcy discharge papers (if applicable)

5. INELIGIBLE APPLICANTS
==========================

5.1 Automatic Decline Criteria
-------------------------------
Applications will be automatically declined if ANY of the following conditions exist:

  - Active bankruptcy (Chapter 7 or 13) not yet discharged
  - Foreclosure within the past 7 years
  - Open tax lien of any amount
  - Outstanding judgment exceeding $5,000
  - Current delinquency on any existing loan (30+ days past due)
  - FICO score below 500
  - DTI ratio exceeding 55% (no exceptions)
  - Applicant is a minor (under 18)
  - Fraudulent information detected on application
  - Applicant on OFAC SDN list or other sanctions list
  - Unverifiable identity or Social Security Number
  - Less than 6 months of credit history (unless using non-traditional credit)
  - Existing debt-to-income exceeds 60% including proposed loan

5.2 Waiting Periods After Adverse Events
------------------------------------------
Chapter 7 Bankruptcy Discharge: 4 years from discharge date
Chapter 13 Bankruptcy Discharge: 2 years from discharge date
Chapter 13 Bankruptcy Dismissal: 4 years from dismissal date
Foreclosure: 7 years from completion date
Deed-in-Lieu of Foreclosure: 4 years from execution date
Short Sale: 4 years from completion date
Multiple Bankruptcies: 5 years from most recent discharge date

6. CO-SIGNER AND GUARANTOR REQUIREMENTS
=========================================

6.1 Co-Signer Eligibility
---------------------------
  - Minimum FICO: 700
  - Maximum DTI: 35%
  - Minimum annual income: $36,000
  - No adverse credit events in the past 7 years
  - Must be a US citizen or permanent resident
  - Must not be co-signer on more than 2 other active loans
  - Must provide all standard income and identity documentation

6.2 Co-Signer Rights and Responsibilities
-------------------------------------------
  - Co-signer is equally responsible for loan repayment
  - Loan appears on co-signer's credit report
  - Late payments affect co-signer's credit history
  - Co-signer receives all loan-related correspondence
  - Co-signer can request release after 24 consecutive on-time payments (subject to credit review)
  - Release requires primary borrower to meet credit and income requirements independently

7. SPECIAL ELIGIBILITY PROGRAMS
================================

7.1 First-Time Homebuyer Program
---------------------------------
  - First-time homebuyer defined: Has not owned a home in the past 3 years
  - Maximum annual income: $120,000 (may vary by MSA)
  - Homebuyer education course completion required (8 hours minimum)
  - Reduced down payment options available (as low as 3%)
  - Reduced PMI rates for qualifying borrowers
  - Closing cost assistance available up to $5,000

7.2 Low-to-Moderate Income (LMI) Program
------------------------------------------
  - Maximum annual income: 80% of area median income (AMI)
  - Minimum FICO reduced by 20 points (not below 580)
  - DTI limit increased by 5 percentage points
  - Financial counseling required for Grade D and below
  - Maximum loan amount: $35,000 (personal), $250,000 (mortgage)

7.3 Employee Internal Loan Program
------------------------------------
  - Eligible after 12 months of continuous employment
  - Minimum FICO: 600 (relaxed from standard 640)
  - Interest rate: Prime rate only (no risk spread applied)
  - Maximum loan amount: $25,000
  - Automatic payroll deduction for payments
  - No prepayment penalty
